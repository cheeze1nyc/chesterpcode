ABOUT

Mercedes-Benz is currently involved in Formula One as a constructor under the name of Mercedes-AMG Petronas Motorsport.

Mercedes has become one of the most successful teams in recent Formula One history, having achieved consecutive Constructors' Championships from 2014 to 2017, and consecutive Drivers' Championships from 2014 to 2018. It has also housed legendary racer, seven-time World Formula Champion Michael Schumacher.

Current drivers include five-time World Formula One Champion, Lewis Hamilton, and two-time Formala 3 Champion, and GP3 Series Champion, Valtteri Bottas.
